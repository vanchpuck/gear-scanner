ICE AXES & CRAMPONS

NORTH COULOIR

Technical ice axe designed for ice climbing and technical mountaineering with fully modular head for adapting the ice axe to all the various activities.

DRON +

Multipurpose ice axe for couloirs, classic slopes on north faces and ridges with mixed climbing.

HOUND +

Classic ice axe for glacier travel, couloirs, technical ski touring and ridges with mixed climbing.

ALPIN TOUR +

Classic ice axe with grip that is ergonomic and moulded, with excellent grip and handling for greater efficiency of use.

ALPIN TOUR

Robust general walking axe with hardened steel cataphoresis black head and tapered anodized light alloy shaft.

ALPIN TOUR L

Lightweight ice axe for classic alpinism and ski touring, entirely made from anodized light alloy.

AGILE

Ice axe for ski-touring competition that is weightless, compact, strong and entirely made form anodized light alloy.

AGILE +

Ice axe for alpinism and ski-touring extremely versatile, compact, strong and equipped with anodized light alloy shaft, hardened steel head and stainless steel spike.

AGILE KIT

Snow safety system for mountaineering, ski-touring and glacier travel.

AGILE KIT +

Snow safety system for mountaineering, ski-touring and glacier travel.

SNOW BLADE

Aluminium snow shovel for ski touring and mountaineering, with an ergonomic handle for better grip during use and improved efficiency during excavation.

HYPER-SPIKE

New 13-point technical crampons, hybrid and modular with interchangeable front points, designed to deal with the hardest and steepest ice walls and mixed climbing in the world and to adapt for the necessities in every situation.

LYCAN

Technical twelve point crampons that are designed for technical mountaineering such as goulottes and north faces. An ideal piece of equipment for those who want to approach the world of icefalls and want to have a versatile multipurpose tool to accompany them in many disciplines

NUPTSE EVO

12 point crampons made from tempered painted steel with new double lever system for size adjustment, easy to operate with one hand only.

NEVIS FLEX

Ten point crampons ideal for the classic lines on glacial prevalence and without steep slopes.

SNOW FLEX

10-point crampons designed for ski touring and progression on snow.

ICE

Twelve point crampons for classic mountaineering and glacier travel. Its technical shape enables an easier ascent of NORTH COULOIRs and crossing ridges with mixed climbing.

MINI CRAMPON 6P

6 point crampons for low grade winter hikes provide extra grip on iced and snowy footpaths.

MINI CRAMPON 4P

4 point crampons for low grade winter hikes provide extra grip on iced and snowy footpaths.

ICE TRACTION +

Ten-point anti-slip hiking crampons with hew stainless steel chains and points with joints and an anti-balling system.

SWHIPPY

Bicolour elastic sling with dual arm with carabiners, equipped with a small swivel that prevents the slings twisting and designed for ice climbing without a leash, making it impossible to lose the ice axe;

WHIPPY

Bicolor elastic webbing 13 mm, designed for leashless ice climbing, it prevents the risk of dropping your ice tool.

FINDER 240

Light alloy probe composed of six sections Ø 12 mm. Quick tensioning system with Kevlar cord.

SNOW ANCHOR

Light alloy tubular snow anchor with steel wire cable connection..

NORTH COULOIR ICE

Spare pick for the NORTH COULOIR ice axes, ideal for ice climbing.

NORTH COULOIR MIXTE

Accessory pick for the NORTH COULOIR ice axes, ideal for use on mixed climbing.

IMPACT HAMMER

Additional weights for the NORTH COULOIR ice axes that are to be installed on to the pick to increase the impact of the ice axe.

NORTH COULOIR HAMMER

Compatible hammer for the NORTH COULOIR ice axes and it enables you to place pitons without compromising the balance of the ice axe.

NORTH COULOIR ADZE

Compatible shovel for the NORTH COULOIR ice axes and it enables you to remove and dig in the snow.

CHANGE OVER

Support for changing hands for the NORTH COULOIR ice axes.

BUMPER

Lower support for both hand and finger protection, compatible with the following ice axes: NORTH COULOIR and DRON+.

DRAG-TOUR

Adjustable ice axe leash.

HEAD COVER

Head cover protection, intended for protecting your gear during transportation, designed to fit onto any ice axe.

PICK COVER

Pick cover protection, intended for protecting your gear during transportation, designed to fit onto any ice axe.

SPIKE COVER

Spike cover protection, intended for protecting your gear during transportation, designed to fit onto any ice axe.

AGILE PICK COVER

AGILE and AGILE + pick cover, useful as replacement for AGILE KIT e AGILE KIT +.

SHOVEL

Replacement shovel for AGILE KIT and AGILE KIT +.

HYPER-SPIKE HOOK

Spare front point of HYPER-SPIKE crampon. It can be mounted in pairs to get dual-point configuration for use on icefalls with classic difficulty.

HYPER-SPIKE BLADE

This removable accessory point is laterally mounted onto mono-point of HYPER-SPIKE crampon, ideal for a technical mixed route or dry-tool training.

HYPER-SPIKE BAR

Spare bar compatible with HYPER-SPIKE crampons.

ANTIBOTT

Spare antibotts with abrasion resistant.

LONG BAR

Bar for crampons that allows to extend the crampons size up to number EU 50 / US 14.

SPARE BAR

Spare bar for crampons.

CRAMPON BAG

Carrying bag suitable to contain crampons, made of strong fabric, with Velcro closer and opening rings.
